# Architecture Metamodel

## Purpose
Define the structure and relationships between architecture elements used in this case study.

## Core Elements
- Business Drivers  
- Business Goals  
- Value Streams  
- Business Capabilities  
- Processes  
- Applications  
- Data Domains  
- Technology Platforms  
- Principles  
- Standards  

## Key Relationships
- Business Drivers influence Business Goals  
- Business Goals drive Value Streams  
- Value Streams are enabled by Capabilities  
- Capabilities are supported by Applications  
- Applications use Data Domains  
- Applications run on Technology Platforms  
- All elements are governed by Principles and Standards  

## Benefits
- Consistent modeling  
- Traceability  
- Clear governance
