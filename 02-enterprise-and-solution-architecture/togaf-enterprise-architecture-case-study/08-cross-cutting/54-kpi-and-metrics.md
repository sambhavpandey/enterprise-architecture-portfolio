# KPI and Metrics Framework

## Purpose
Measure success of the transformation.

## Business KPIs
- Time-to-market  
- Revenue growth  
- Customer satisfaction  
- Operational efficiency  

## IT KPIs
- Application count reduction  
- Infrastructure cost  
- System availability  
- Automation coverage  

## Governance KPIs
- Architecture compliance rate  
- Review cycle time  
- Standard adoption rate  

## Measurement Approach
- Quarterly reviews  
- Executive dashboards  
- Continuous improvement loop
