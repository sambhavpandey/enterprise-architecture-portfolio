# Architecture Principles

## Business Principles
- Business drives architecture  
- Value realization over technology adoption  
- Standardization before customization  

## Data Principles
- Data is an enterprise asset  
- Single source of truth  
- Data quality by design  

## Application Principles
- Modular and loosely coupled  
- API-first design  
- Reuse before buy, buy before build  

## Technology Principles
- Cloud-first  
- Security by design  
- Automation by default  

## Governance Principles
- Architecture compliance is mandatory  
- Transparency in decisions  
- Continuous improvement
