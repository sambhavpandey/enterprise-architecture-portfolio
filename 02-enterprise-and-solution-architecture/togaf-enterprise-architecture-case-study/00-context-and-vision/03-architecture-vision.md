# Architecture Vision

## Vision Statement
Build a unified, business-driven enterprise architecture that enables growth, efficiency, resilience, and innovation through standardized, scalable, and governed technology platforms.

## Objectives
- Align business strategy and technology  
- Reduce fragmentation and duplication  
- Enable analytics and automation  
- Improve governance and control  

## Scope

### In Scope
- Business, data, application, and technology domains  
- Global operations  

### Out of Scope
- Product-level detailed design  

## Success Criteria
- Measurable cost reduction  
- Faster delivery cycles  
- Improved visibility and control  
- Strong governance adoption
