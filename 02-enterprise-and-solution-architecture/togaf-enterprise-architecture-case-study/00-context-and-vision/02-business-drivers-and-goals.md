# Business Drivers and Goals

## Business Drivers
- Global expansion into emerging markets  
- Rising competition and pricing pressure  
- High IT operational cost  
- Slow product innovation cycles  
- Increasing regulatory requirements  

## Strategic Goals
- Reduce IT cost by 25–30%  
- Improve time-to-market by 40%  
- Enable data-driven decisions  
- Improve operational resilience  
- Establish architecture governance  

## Business to Architecture Mapping
- Growth → Scalable and modular architecture  
- Efficiency → Rationalized applications and cloud-first model  
- Innovation → Data platform and AI-ready design  
- Compliance → Embedded governance and security
