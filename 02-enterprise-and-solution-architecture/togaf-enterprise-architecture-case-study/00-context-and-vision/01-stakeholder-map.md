# Stakeholder Map

## Purpose
Identify key stakeholders, their interests, influence, and engagement approach for the enterprise architecture transformation.

## Stakeholder Groups

### Executive Leadership
- CEO – Business growth and market expansion  
- CIO – Technology modernization and cost control  
- CFO – Financial efficiency, ROI, risk control  
- COO – Operational efficiency and resilience  

### Business Leadership
- Business Unit Heads – Faster delivery and flexibility  
- Sales and Marketing – Customer experience and agility  
- Manufacturing and Supply Chain – Efficiency and visibility  

### IT Leadership
- Enterprise Architecture Team – Alignment and governance  
- Application Teams – Delivery and modernization  
- Infrastructure and Security – Stability and compliance  

### External Stakeholders
- Technology Vendors  
- System Integrators  
- Regulatory Bodies  

## Influence vs Interest Matrix

### High Influence, High Interest
- CEO, CIO, CFO, COO  

### High Influence, Low Interest
- Board, Regulators  

### Low Influence, High Interest
- Business Managers, Delivery Teams  

### Low Influence, Low Interest
- External Partners  

## Engagement Strategy
- Executives – Monthly steering meetings and roadmap reviews  
- Business Leaders – Workshops and capability mapping sessions  
- IT Teams – Architecture forums and design reviews  
- External – Contractual and compliance reviews
