# Business Capability Map

## Purpose
Define what the business must be able to do to achieve its strategy.

## Capability Domains

### Customer Domain
- Customer management
- Sales management
- Marketing management
- Customer support

### Product Domain
- Product strategy
- Product design
- Engineering
- Lifecycle management

### Operations Domain
- Manufacturing
- Supply chain
- Logistics
- Quality management

### Finance Domain
- Accounting
- Budgeting
- Financial reporting
- Compliance

### People Domain
- Talent management
- Payroll
- Learning and development

### IT Domain
- Enterprise architecture
- Application management
- Infrastructure and security
- Data and analytics

## Observations
- Overlapping capabilities across regions  
- Inconsistent maturity levels
