# Value Stream Map

## Purpose
Define how business value is created from idea to customer and post-sale service.

## Core Value Streams

### Idea-to-Product
- Market research
- Product concept
- Design and engineering
- Testing and validation
- Product launch

### Order-to-Cash
- Lead generation
- Sales and quoting
- Order processing
- Fulfillment
- Invoicing and payment

### Procure-to-Pay
- Supplier selection
- Purchase requisition
- Purchase order
- Goods receipt
- Invoice and payment

### Plan-to-Produce
- Demand planning
- Production planning
- Manufacturing
- Quality control
- Warehouse and dispatch

### Service-to-Resolution
- Customer issue logging
- Diagnosis
- Resolution
- Feedback and closure

## Key Issues
- Process variation across regions  
- Manual handoffs  
- Limited end-to-end visibility  

## Improvement Goals
- Standardized flows  
- Automation of handoffs  
- Real-time visibility
