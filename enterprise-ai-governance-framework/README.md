# Enterprise AI Governance & Assurance Framework

This repository presents an enterprise-ready AI governance and assurance framework
inspired by ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management System).

The framework demonstrates how AI governance can be operationalized by:
- Integrating ISO/IEC 42001 with ISO/IEC 27001 and SOC 2
- Embedding AI risk management into existing security and compliance controls
- Using cloud-native services (Azure) for technical enforcement
- Maintaining audit-ready evidence and traceability

## What this is
- An implementation-focused interpretation of ISO/IEC 42001
- A practical AI Management System (AIMS) operating model
- A portfolio-quality reference for enterprise AI governance

## What this is NOT
- Official ISO documentation
- Certification material
- Client-specific or proprietary content

## Key Topics Covered
- AI scope definition and organizational context
- Leadership accountability and AI policy
- AI risk and impact assessments
- AI lifecycle governance and operations
- Performance monitoring and continual improvement
- Evidence and audit readiness

## Intended Audience
- Enterprise Architects
- Security and Risk Leaders
- AI Governance and Compliance Teams
- Cloud and Platform Architects
